Roller Shutters

We understand that security is paramount and feeling safe in your home is key, that is why we have partenered with Roller Shutter Rescue to give our clients high quality Roller Shutters. We offer two types of operation:
  • Electric
  • Manual
Both operating systems are backed with a warranty, as they should be. However we do specialise in 240 volt Electric Roller Shutters as we have found over the past decade that they have proven to be more reliable:
  • They are hard-wired into your house power
  • Wireless Handsets which can control multiple Roller Shutters even all at once (NO MORE HOLES THROUGH WALLS!)
  • Guaranteed for 5 years (because we know that they will last the Australian conditions)
  • Over their lifetime they will cost less than replacing batteries in inferior 12 volt systems or manual system components
  • Our motors have Brass gears NOT plastic
However, we can and will do 12v and Manual operation to suit the customer's needs.

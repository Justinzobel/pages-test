Vertical Blinds

We understand that not every window is the same, with the new designs of windows today we know that we have to be able to manufacture Vertical Blinds for a variety of applications:
  • Left Hand Stacks (for windows that open Right to Left)
  • Right Hand Stacks (for windows that open Left to Right)
  • Centre Opening (for windows and sliding doors that open from the middle)
  • Centre Stacks (for windows that open left and right to sit in the middle)

We also understand that most indivduals have pets or young children so it is of utmost importanct that hazards of blinds are minimalized that is why we have introduced a few new features:
